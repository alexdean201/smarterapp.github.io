Implementation Readiness

The resources on this page are what the Smarter Balanced team have been identified as necessary to implement Smarter Balanced Assessments are what comprise the Implementation Readiness Package (IRP).

The resources presented should be used to establish foundational understanding as well as provide technical and functional information related to the implementation and delivery of Smarter Balanced Assessments. We suggest accessing the Overview Document as the starting point to leveraging the IRP resources.

Other Considerations
Members may require additional services that are essential to administering assessments that may need to be performed by service providers. Smarter Balanced is unable to specifically provide a comprehensive list of these services as they vary by Member, but we recommend that appropriate internal evaluation and consideration be given to those potential services.

Examples of services include, but are not limited to:

  • Help and/or Service desk operation
  • User account management, creation, and support
    • Ancillary services like email
  • Operational functions such as managing, storing, and transmitting both sensitive and non-sensitive student data
  • Managing access to Smarter Balanced resources
  • Change management
  • Hand-scoring mechanisms
  • Data integration
  • Consortium specific duties
    • Attending in-person and web-based conference meetings
